    AnvilKanban

    A practical multi-tool workspace for Windows.

    Kanban boards, whiteboards, diagrams, code editing, and more — all in one dockable desktop app. Everything runs locally. No subscriptions, no cloud, no tracking.

    Download for Windows See all features

    Everything you need, in one place

    • Kanban Board

      Visual task management with swim lanes, backlog, and archive. Drag, prioritize, and ship.

    • Excalidraw

      Hand-drawn style diagrams and whiteboards. Sketch ideas fast, export anywhere.

    • Draw.io

      Professional flowcharts, UML, network diagrams, and more with hundreds of shape libraries.

    • Monaco Editor

      The editor behind VS Code. Full syntax highlighting for 100+ languages, right in your workspace.

    • Knowledge Base

      A searchable, linked document library. Capture and connect ideas without leaving your workflow.

    • Calendar

      Plan and track work over time. Linked to your Kanban cards and project milestones.

    • Dashboard

      At-a-glance summaries of your active projects, recent work, and team activity.

    • Document Browser

      Browse, search, and open project documents without leaving the app.

    Why AnvilKanban?

    Private by default

    All your data stays on your machine. No accounts, no sync servers, no cloud storage. SQLite files you own and control.

    Works offline

    Every tool works without an internet connection. Your workflow never depends on a third-party service being up.

    Flexible workspace

    Dockable panels let you arrange any combination of tools side by side. Save layouts per project.

    VS Code inspired

    Dark and light themes that match your development environment. Familiar shortcuts, familiar feel.
